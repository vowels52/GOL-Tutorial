What is Multi Factor Authentication (MFA)?

Multi-factor authentication means using two things instead of one to prove it's really you.

Here's the easiest way to think about it:

  • Step 1: You enter your password.
  • Step 2: Your phone gives you a little code to make sure it's truly you.

That's it!

It's like using both a key and a quick "secret code" to get into your house. Much safer, but still easy once you get the hang of it.
